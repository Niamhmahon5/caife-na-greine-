Who We Are

Caife na Gréine, meaning "Coffee of the Sun" in Irish, was founded with one simple goal — to create a warm, welcoming space where people can slow down, connect, and enjoy truly great coffee.

We source our beans from sustainable farms and bake our pastries fresh every morning. Whether you're working remotely or catching up with a friend, our doors are always open.

Cúpla Focal — A Few Words in Irish

As an Irish café, we love celebrating our language. Here are some useful phrases to know:

Caife, le do thoil A coffee, please
Go raibh maith agat Thank you
Tá sé go hálainn It is beautiful
Caife na Gréine Coffee of the Sun
Fáilte romhat You are welcome
Slán go fóill Goodbye for now
